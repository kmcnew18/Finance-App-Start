Common questions

Is Arko actually free?

Yes — manual budgeting, the full ledger, and dashboard tracking are free forever, no card required and no time limit. You only pay if you want connected accounts and the automated features on top.

What happens when my trial ends?

Every new account gets a 14-day trial of full access. If you don't upgrade before it ends, your account drops to the free Manual tier automatically — any connected accounts are disconnected (fully, at Plaid's end too), but nothing you entered manually is ever touched.

Do I have to connect a bank account?

No. Manual tracking works completely on its own — connecting accounts is there for when you want balances and transactions to update themselves instead of typing them in.

Is my financial data safe?

Account connections go through Plaid, the same infrastructure most banking apps use — your bank login is never seen or stored by Arko. Read-only access by default, encrypted connections throughout, and you can disconnect any account at any time.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans cancel from Settings whenever you want — you keep access through the end of what you already paid for, and you're never charged again after that. Lifetime plans are a one-time payment with nothing to cancel.

What's the difference between the paid tiers?

Automation adds bank and card connections with auto-syncing balances, up to 6 accounts. Full adds investment and debt account linking, up to 15 accounts, spending trends, visual savings goals, and the investment portfolio view.
